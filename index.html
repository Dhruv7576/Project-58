Rubik's cube is my favourite hobby. There are six sides in a rubik's cube. Each side has one colour. We have to mix all the colours and solve them. There are many different types of rubik's cubes. For example 3X3, 4X4, 5X5, 6X6, Pyramid cube etc.My fastest record of the 3X3 rubik's cube is 35 seconds.
